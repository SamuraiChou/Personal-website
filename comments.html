Technical Aspects Of The Website

The website was constructed using HTML and then syled usung a stylesheet. HTML elements are composed of basic tags like HTML, Head and Body, which require closing tags, and this website adheres to that structure. The website also includes divs with classes that segregate the elements into meaningful sections, making it easy for myself to identify which parts I am editing and what information I am inserting. The structure of the website can be categorized as a "one column layout" with two additional columns represented by red bars on the sides. The table below provides details on the functions of the tags or classes used and the reasons for their inclusion.

Aesthetics Of The Website

When designing the website, my approach was to create a representation of my interests. Initially, I aimed for a simplistic design with soothing colors, but later I changed my mind and opted for a neon theme inspired by the vibrant color palettes used in TikTok, as they are exciting and lively. Although I didnt completely achieve the look I had pictured initially, it nontheless turned out well. It is not anything too flashy or chaotic with no gimmicky elements being used which reflects the simplicity of the website. The colour palette consists of a black, light-red, cyan and white. I used a black background with white text as I am a fan of dark mode. The colour cyan was used for certain headings and the navigation bar and the light-red was used for borders. Certain gaming/movie references are used across the website reflecting my interests and adding to that neon aesthetic .Overall, I do think the website accompliched my goal of making it an exciting and vibrant aesthetic.

The font sizes are thoughtfully chosen to ensure that the audience does not strain their eyes. The size varies depending on whether it is a heading or a paragraph, allowing for clear differentiation in the importance of information. The footer's navigation bar has a smaller font size as I did not wish for it to take up to much space however it serves as a convenient way for users to navigate to other webpages without scrolling to the top. The line-height is set to enhance readability, and the font type "Trebuchet MS" is chosen as I believe it to be a good fit for the website.

Accessibility Aspects Of The Website

The language setting of the website is English to ensure easy discoverability through English keywords on search engines like Google. All images are accompanied by alternative text, providing assistance to visually impaired users, and they also have titles that provide additional information when hovered over. The top image, displayed as a background through CSS, does not have alternative text, but a title and transparent text on the top left are included as fallback options in case the image does not load. Hovering over buttons in the navigation bar on the header or footer displays the hyperlink destination of the respective page. The current page button is also underlined to indicate to users that they are currently on that particular page.
